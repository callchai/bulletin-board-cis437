Posting

Click the Post button. Type your message, and click to post. You cannot edit once it is posted. Posting with an empty square will cancel the post.

Your Identity

The Board's divinity grants you a new identity upon the board to reinvent yourself on your first visit. You are assigned a color as well. If you return, you will keep your identity. You may think your new identity and newfound anonymity is a license to sin, but remember the commandments you agreed to.

Board Resets

The board resets on a schedule. The lifespan clock shows how long the current board has been alive. When it resets, all posts are cleared.
